About

Real-time 2D rigid-body simulation in Matter.js. The hull is a chain of 24 rigid segments joined by breakable constraints. Flooding pressure (Torricelli), buoyancy, and CoM shift are computed each step and applied as forces to the engine bodies. Break location and time, funnel falls, and the time multiplier all emerge from the solver — none are scripted.
